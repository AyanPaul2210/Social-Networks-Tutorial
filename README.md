# Social-Networks-Tutorial
# 🌐 Social Networks Tutorials (NPTEL-Based)

A complete 12-week Social Networks tutorial, featuring concept-wise presentations and analytical examples, plus a revision module.  
Covers network basics, weak ties, homophily, structural balance, PageRank, diffusion, power laws, epidemics, and small-world networks.  
Ideal for structured, concept-driven learning.

---

## 📘 Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive 12-week tutorial series on **Social Networks**, curated in alignment with the **NPTEL course (noc26-cs82)** structure.  
It includes 12 weeks of detailed presentations covering both foundational and advanced topics, followed by a **revision module**, along with **well-structured analytical examples and visual explanations**.

---

## 📚 What’s Inside

- 🎓 Week-wise Presentations (PPTs)  
  Each week focuses on a specific theme in Social Networks, including:
  - Network fundamentals and graph representations  
  - Social network datasets and connectedness  
  - Strength of weak ties and structural holes  
  - Homophily, triadic closure, and network formation  
  - Structural balance and segregation models  
  - Web graph and PageRank algorithms  
  - Diffusion, cascades, and information spread  
  - Power laws and network robustness  
  - Epidemic spreading models  
  - Small-world networks and decentralized search  
  - Influence maximization and core nodes  

- 🧠 Revision Week  
  A final module summarizing key concepts, models, and analytical insights from all weeks.

- 📊 Analytical Examples & Visualizations  
  Concept-driven examples to build intuition for network behavior, influence, and dynamics.
